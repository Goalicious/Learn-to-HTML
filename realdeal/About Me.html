I also like to cook. Would you like to see one of my favorite recipies?

Kirschmichel

You need the following things:
  • 1kg pitted Cherries
  • 250g flour
  • 250ml milk
  • 4 eggs
  • A pinch of salt
  • About 150g of butter
  • About100g of sugar

After gathering the ingredients and pitting the cherries proceed with the following steps:
  1. Turn on the oven at 190°C
  2. Mix flour, milk, eggs and salt
  3. Melt the butter and pour ¾ of it in your baking dish
  4. Spread half of the sugar on the bottom of the dish
  5. Pour the dough slowly in the dish
  6. Now it's time for the cherries - let them sink in for a moment
  7. Put the remaining butter and sugar on top
  8. Bake for about 30 Minutes and serve hot
A green salad with buttermilk dressing fits very nicely.
